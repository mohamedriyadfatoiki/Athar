Welcome to Athar

Some words are never spoken.
Some goodbyes never feel complete.

Athar was created in memory of a dear friend who passed away. May Allah have mercy on his soul. It has since become a place for anyone carrying the memory of someone they deeply love.

Whether you miss a friend, a parent, a sibling, a child, or anyone whose absence still echoes in your heart, this is a space for you.

Write the words you never had the chance to say. Share a cherished memory. Offer a heartfelt du'a. Or simply let your thoughts rest here, where love and remembrance live on.

Because while life comes to an end, love does not. And as long as we remember them, pray for them, and carry their legacy in our hearts, a part of them remains with us.

May every message be a prayer.
May every memory become an everlasting Athar.
